# Shared Road Material Research — Class 6 vs RAP

A research and decision memo prepared by Shaun Anderson & Trevor Ekstrom
(For the Grader Good, LLC) for our shared community road in rural
Jefferson County, Colorado.

**Live page:** https://swiftbird.github.io/road-research/

Documents the research, sources, and reasoning behind the choice of
Class 6 Aggregate Base Course over recycled asphalt for the Spring 2026
resurfacing.
